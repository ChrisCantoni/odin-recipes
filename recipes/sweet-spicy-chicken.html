Odin's Sweet and Spicy Gochujang Chicken

Description

Gochujang is a delicious and savory Korean hot pepper paste, perfect to bring your sad little chicken cutlets to life with flavor and originality! The best thing about this recipe is that it's easy and tasty. Pretty soon you'll be a Gochujang culinary extraordinare!

Ingredients List

  • 1/3 cup Gochujang
  • 4 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp sweet rice wine (mirin)
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 3 cloves of garlic, grated
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp freshly grated ginger
  • 1 tbsp canola oil
  • 2 pounds skinless, boneless, chicken thighs, cut into bite-size pieces
  • 1.5 tsp toasted sesame seeds
  • 3 tbsp thinly sliced green onions
  • 6 cups cooked rice

Steps

  1. Gather your ingredients like a champion
  2. Stir together the gochujang, soy sauce, sweet rice wine, honey, garlic, sesame oil, and ginger in a small bowl.
  3. Heat oil over medium-high heat in a wok or a large skillet. Add half of the chicken to the wok. Cook and stirt until the chicken is no longer pink. About 5 minutes.
  4. Remove the chicken from the wok. Repeat with the uncooked chicken. Then put all the chicken back in the wok.
  5. Stir the sauce well, then add it to the wok, cooking and stirring until the sauce has thickened and become bubbly. About 3 minutes.
  6. Top with the sesame seeds and green onions and serve immediately with rice. Tell your family you had to travel all the way to Seoul to learn the intricacies of this rare Korean delicacy. They'll never know how easy it truly was!
